? WORKFLOW: FIND & OPEN SOLUTION (< 5 seconds)                   ?
??????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????
? 1. Launch Solution Opener                                      ?
? 2. Press Ctrl+F (or just start typing in search box)           ?
? 3. Type 2-3 letters of solution name                           ?
? 4. Press Enter                                                 ?
? 5. Visual Studio opens!                                        ?
??????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????

??????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????
? WORKFLOW: ADD REPOSITORY                                       ?
??????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????
? 1. Click "? Add Repository" or press Ctrl+N                   ?
? 2. Browse to repository folder                                 ?
? 3. Click "Select Folder"                                       ?
? 4. Wait for scan to complete                                   ?
? 5. New tab appears with all solutions                          ?

??????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????
? No solutions found    ? Check folder path, press F5            ?
? Solution won't open   ? Verify VS is installed                 ?
? Too many results      ? Add more specific search terms         ?
? Lost configuration    ? Check config.json.bak file             ?
? Window off-screen     ? Delete config.json to reset            ?

??????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????
? • First scan may take 30-60s for large repos (1000+ solutions) ?
? • Results are cached - no rescanning on app restart            ?
? • Search is instant even with thousands of solutions           ?
? • Only refresh (F5) when you pull new code                     ?
