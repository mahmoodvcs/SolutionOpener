### Button Animations
- **Hover**: Smooth color change + shadow (150ms)

- **Search Box**: Border color + thickness change
- **Tabs**: Underline slide in/out

### Loading States
- **Progress Bar**: Smooth indeterminate animation
- **Overlay**: Fade in/out
